Space Crash

Space Crash is a 1 month long project to make along Game designer a Puzzle Game in Vr using Unreal engine 5.

The game is about a spaceship that has crashed on an unknown planet. The player has to solve puzzles to repair the spaceship and escape the planet. You can check out the game on GitHub

Description:

Space Crash is a VR Horror puzzle game that takes place on an alien planet. The player is tasked with repairing their spaceship and escaping the planet by solving a series of puzzles. The game features a variety of puzzles, each with its own unique challenges and solutions. Players must use their wits and problem-solving skills to overcome obstacles and progress through the game. With immersive VR gameplay and stunning visuals, Space Crash offers an exciting and engaging experience for players of all ages.

Features:

  • VR Gameplay: Immerse yourself in a virtual reality experience that puts you in the heart of the action
  • Horror Elements: Experience a thrilling horror atmosphere as you explore the alien planet and encounter mysterious creatures
  • Puzzle Solving: Use your problem-solving skills to repair your spaceship and escape the planet
  • Alien Encounters: Beware of the alien creatures that inhabit the planet, as they pose a threat to your survival
  • Stunning Visuals: Enjoy breathtaking graphics and detailed environments that bring the game world to life
