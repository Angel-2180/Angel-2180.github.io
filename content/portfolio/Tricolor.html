Tricolor

Tricolor is a 4 days GameJam game developed in Unity. This game was based on the theme of 1 billion consumers.

We wanted to make a game about garbage collection and recycling based on the theme of 1 billion consumers. With that idea in mind we settled for an experience where you take the roles of a garbage collector in a recycling center where all hell break loose with the massive influx of garbage that are to be sorted and recycled. You can check out the game on GitHub

Description:

In the quick-paced game "Tricolor," which tests your reflexes and coordination, you'll encounter the mayhem of a recycling center. Sorting and recycling different materials is a necessary for waste collectors in order to maintain the facility's efficiency. Take caution, since the level of difficulty is rising with time. Are you able to take the heat and emerge as the ultimate recycling victor?

Features:

  • Fast-Paced Gameplay: Engage in a high-energy experience that challenges your reflexes and coordination
  • Recycling Challenges: Sort and recycle various materials to maintain the recycling center's efficiency
  • Increasing Difficulty: As time progresses, the game becomes more challenging, testing your ability to adapt and respond quickly
  • Unexpected Waste: Watch out for cats sneaking into the sorting center; you may need to call the animal protection truck.
  • Environmental Awareness: Learn about the importance of recycling and waste management in a fun and engaging way
