Makes Racing Great Again

Makes Racing Great Again is a racing game developed in Unreal Engine 5. This game was a collaboration between programmers and artists to create a fun and engaging experience.

The game's objective was to be a modified version of Mario Kart. The game has a distinct track design and a wide range of power-ups. We are working on multiplayer support, and there is AI that may be played against as well. The game is still in development and will be released soon. You can check out the game on GitHub.

Description:

Experience the resurgence of thrilling racing action in "Make Racing Great Again," a dynamic game that captures the excitement of the track. Developed using cutting-edge Unreal Engine 5 and C++, immerse yourself in the adrenaline-fueled world of high-speed competition as you navigate through exhilarating courses and outmaneuver your opponents.

Features:

  • Patriotic Aesthetic: Embrace the spirit of American racing with a visually stunning design that pays homage to the nation's heritage, featuring iconic landmarks and patriotic motifs.
  • Dynamic Power-ups: Equip yourself with a range of power-ups to gain the upper hand on the racetrack. From speed boosts to tactical maneuvers, strategic item usage is key to securing victory.
  • Competitive AI: Test your skills against computer-controlled opponents who are determined to reclaim the glory of American racing. With varying levels of difficulty, every race presents a new challenge.
  • Exciting Tracks: Race across a diverse array of tracks, each with its own unique challenges and surprises. Discover hidden shortcuts and master the art of precision driving to secure your place on the podium.
  • Multiplayer Thrills (Coming Soon): Prepare to challenge friends and rivals alike in intense multiplayer showdowns. Stay tuned for upcoming updates as we work to integrate multiplayer functionality into the game.
